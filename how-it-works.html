How Rent2Buy Works

At Rent2BuyCars.co.nz, we offer a flexible and affordable option for you to rent a car with the possibility of purchasing it at the end of your rental period. Here’s how it works:

  1. Pick Your Car: Browse our listings of high-quality cars available for rent with the option to buy.
  2. Rent the Car: Choose the rental term that works best for you and enjoy driving your new car.
  3. Buy the Car: At the end of your rental term, you can choose to buy the car for a discounted price or return it with no further obligation.

It’s that simple! Ready to get started?
